Sports

      

Games are not about the trophies and medals; games are about the fun and enjoyment.... S.N Hall follow this principle. S.N Hall provides facilities for playing indoor and outdoor games for the residents. It has a games room for playing indoor games.
For playing outdoor games the hall is having:
1. One volley ball court
2. Two badmintons court
3. One basket ball court
The sports room remains open round the clock on all the days. The residents can get sport materials for a limited period on issue/return basis from the sports secretary of the hall.

Instructions/Rules:
1. The issued sport item should be returned within time.
2. The sport item must be submitted even if the item is broken.
3. A fine will be imposed if anyone fails to return/ reissue it on the due date.
4. The issuer is completely responsible for the issued sport item.
5. Disussions/ Meetings/ Private talks are not allowed inside the reding room without any prior permission.
6. Please switch off te lights, fans, when there is nobody inside the reading room.
